Is your water safe?

With increasing water shortages and undependable supply, more and more people have turned to alternative sources of water however, not all water is safe to drink.

Have confidence in your water

We assess your water quality to determine if it meets the World Healh Organization's standards for drinking water. If it does not, we design a solution suited to your home or organisation to ensure you have safe and healthy water.

Water Quality Assessment

We collect sample of you water and test it for the presence of harmful chemicals and bacteria. We also test for the presence of minerals and other substances that may affect the taste of your water.

System Design

We make recommendations on how your water can be treated to make it safe for drinking or other uses you have for it. We also provide a quote for the installation of the recommended system.

Supply and Fit

We supply and fit the recommended system and provide a maintenance schedule for testing of your water and the efficiency of the system to ensure it continues to work optimally.
